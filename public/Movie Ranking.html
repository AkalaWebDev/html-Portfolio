Ranking My Top 5 Video Games

The Complete List of Akala's Favorite Games of All Time


1. Tetris

The best of the best. The simplicity is absoloutly astounding and so easy to play over and over again.

2. The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

The best adventure game to exist with plenty of combat and puzzle solving to keep you going for hours on end.

3. Halo Combat Evolved

A masterclass in how to create an incredible first person shooter. The level design is beautiful even after releasing over a decade ago. Each map is excellently created. This game is an absolute blast to play.

4. Super Mario Odyssey

Best 3D platforming game to exist. It gives the impression of an open world game while also keeping players within specific areas for each level.

5. Metaphor: ReFantazio

Takes turn based JRPG mechanics and makes it truly exciting to play. The combat is fun and interesting while also giving the player the true feeling of progression and increasing in strength with stats.
